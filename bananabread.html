"Chocolate and Pecan Banana Bread"

Recipe(1 Tin Loaf);


Banana Bread Mix;

  • 200 ml sunflower oil
  • 150 gr Caster Sugar
  • 2 Eggs
  • 250 gr Self-Raising Flour
  • Small pinch of salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking Powder
  • Splash of vanilla extract(1 teaspoon)
  • 100 gr 70% Dark Chocolate(Chopped)
  • 60 gr Chopped Pecan Nuts
  • 100 gr Smashed Banana
  • 100 gr chopped Walnuts

Method;

  1. First of all we start by smashing the bananas and put them aside.
  2. We whisk the eggs and the sugar together vigorously until we have a nice and homogeneus mix
  3. Add in the Sunflower oil in a nice and steady stream (while whisking)
  4. Next go in with your vanilla extract,salt and add in the flour,Nuts and mix (with a wooden spoon or spatula this time)
  5. Last but not least Mix in the banana and chocolate
  6. Pour into the tin and sprinkle the walnuts on top and bake at 160C for 45 Minutes(Make sure its done by inserting a skewer or pointy knife and it comes out nice and clean

BAKING TIP:Melt some white and Dark Chocolate and splash the top of the cake with it once the cake has cooled down for a more chocolatey Finish.
